Our Story

Rooted in Nature, Driven by Science

Founded by Ashwini Pusadkar, a qualified dietician with a postgraduate degree in Food Science & Nutrition from Nagpur University, Marjarry's Nutrizone has spent over 12 years developing and producing holistic food products.

Every product is sourced directly from trusted local farmers and crafted without any artificial additives — because we believe your food should work for you, not against you.
